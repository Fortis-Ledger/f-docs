### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Reputation Staking ‚Äî Trust System

FRT serves as economic collateral for platform participants. This creates a hybrid Web2 + Web3 trust mechanism.

#### How Reputation Staking Works

```
Step 1: Organization stakes 50,000 FRT as collateral
Step 2: Organization hosts tournaments, manages guild
Step 3: If org commits fraud or allows cheating...
Step 4: Stake gets SLASHED (partial or full)
Step 5: Victims compensated from slashed FRT
```

#### Slashing Conditions

| Violation | Slashing % | Appeal |
|:----------|:-----------|:-------|
| **Tournament manipulation** | 100% | No |
| **Allowing cheaters** | 50-100% | Yes (review) |
| **Prize pool theft** | 100% | No |
| **Fraudulent org claims** | 100% | No |
| **Minor rule violations** | 10-25% | Yes |

#### Who Must Stake

| Role | Minimum Stake | Purpose |
|:-----|:--------------|:--------|

<Info>
