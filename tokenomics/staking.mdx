# Staking Guide

> **Stake for Utility, Not Returns. Access, Not Investment.**

Staking in FortisArena is about **unlocking platform features** and demonstrating commitment, not earning financial returns. Both FRT and FPT can be staked for different utility benefits.

<Warning>
  **No Financial Returns**
  
  Staking FortisArena tokens does NOT provide:
  - ‚ùå APY or interest
  - ‚ùå Passive income
  - ‚ùå Investment returns
  - ‚ùå Profit sharing
  
  ‚úÖ Staking gives **platform utility only**
</Warning>

---

## What is Staking Here?

**Traditional DeFi Staking**: Lock tokens ‚Üí Earn more tokens (yield)  
**FortisArena Staking**: Lock tokens ‚Üí Unlock platform features (utility)

By staking, you demonstrate commitment to the platform and gain access to enhanced features, discounts, and exclusive opportunities.

## Managing Your Stakes

### Staking Dashboard

Your staking dashboard shows:

| Metric | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Active Stakes** | Number of active staking positions |
| **Total FRT Staked** | Sum of all FRT locked |
| **Total FPT Staked** | Sum of all FPT locked |
| **Unlocked Features** | List of current benefits active |
| **Lock End Dates** | When each stake can be withdrawn |

### Adding to Stakes

You can create multiple stakes:
- Different tiers for different purposes
- Multiple FRT stakes for different organizations
- Separate FPT stakes for different benefit levels

**Each stake is independent** with its own lock period and benefits.

### Unstaking

#### Normal Unstaking (After Lock Period)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    View your active stakes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Stake">
    Click on matured stake (lock period completed)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate Unstake">
    Click "Unstake" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Submit transaction (gas fee applies)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Tokens">
    Original stake returns to wallet
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Benefits End">
    Unlocked features deactivate
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Early Unstaking (Before Lock Period)

You can unstake early, but there are consequences:

| Early Withdrawal | Penalty | What You Lose |
|:-----------------|:--------|:--------------|
| **FRT Staking** | 20% of staked amount | All unlocked features immediately |
| **FPT Staking** | 10% of staked amount | All perks immediately |

**Penalty Distribution:**
- 100% of penalty is **burned** (deflationary)
- No one receives the penalty amount

<Warning>
  **Think carefully before early unstaking.** You lose both tokens (penalty) and benefits immediately.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I earn more tokens by staking?

**No.** Staking FRT or FPT does NOT generate more tokens. There is no APY, no yield, no interest. You stake to unlock platform features and benefits.

### What's the difference between FRT and FPT staking?

| Aspect | FRT Staking | FPT Staking |
|:-------|:------------|:------------|
| **Purpose** | Access power features | Get convenience perks |
| **Examples** | Org registration, hosting | Fee discounts, cosmetics |
| **Lock Periods** | Longer (3 months - 2 years) | Shorter (flexible - 180 days) |
| **Who Needs It** | Organizations, serious users | All players, creators |

### Can I have multiple stakes?

Yes! You can:
- Stake FRT in multiple tiers for different features
- Stake FPT separately for perks
- Have both FRT and FPT stakes active simultaneously

### What happens when my stake unlocks?

When the lock period ends:
- Benefits continue as long as you keep staked
- You can unstake anytime without penalty
- If you unstake, benefits stop immediately

### Is staking required to use FortisArena?

**No.** Staking is completely optional:
- Basic platform use: No staking required
- Social features: No staking required
- Free tournaments: No staking required
- Advanced features: Staking required

### Can I lose my staked tokens?

Only in these situations:
1. **Early unstaking** ‚Üí 10-20% penalty
2. **Reputation slashing** (FRT only) ‚Üí If your org violates rules
3. **Smart contract hack** ‚Üí Extremely unlikely, but possible

### How is this different from DeFi staking?

| DeFi Staking | FortisArena Staking |
|:-------------|:--------------------|
| Lock tokens | Lock tokens |
| Earn yield/APY | Unlock features |
| Financial return | Utility return |
| Risk of impermanent loss | No LP risk |
| Compound rewards | No compounding |

## Risks & Considerations

### Lock Period Risk

Staked tokens cannot be accessed until the lock period ends (unless you pay the early penalty).

**Mitigation**: 
- Only stake what you don't need immediately
- Use flexible staking for uncertain situations
- Plan your token needs in advance

### Smart Contract Risk

While our contracts are audited, all smart contracts carry some risk.

**Protections**:
- Multiple professional audits
- Bug bounty program
- Emergency pause functionality
- Time-delayed on critical functions

### Benefit Change Risk

Platform benefits associated with staking tiers may change over time.

**Policy**:
- Grandfathering for existing stakes
- 30-day notice for major changes
- Community input on adjustments
