## What is FPT?

FPT (Fortis Points Token) is the **in-platform currency** of FortisArena. It powers daily activities, rewards active participation, and enables micro-transactions across the ecosystem.

Think of FPT as "platform credits" that happen to live on the blockchain ‚Äî earned through gameplay, spent on features, and always tied to real utility.

#### Burn vs Recycle by Category

| Spend Category | Burn % | Recycle % | Destination |
|:---------------|:-------|:----------|:------------|

**Why This Matters:**
- **Burn** = Deflationary pressure on FPT
- **Recycle** = Sustainable reward economy
- **Combined** = Long-term token health

#### How Utility Staking Works

1. **Stake FPT** ‚Üí Lock tokens in smart contract
2. **Unlock Perks** ‚Üí Benefits activate immediately
3. **Maintain Stake** ‚Üí Perks continue while staked
4. **Unstake Anytime** ‚Üí Tokens returned (no penalty)
5. **Perks Lost** ‚Üí Benefits stop upon unstaking
