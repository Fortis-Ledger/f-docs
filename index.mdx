# Welcome to the FortisArena Knowledge Base

FortisArena is pioneering the future of competitive gaming by fully integrating Web3 technology with high-stakes eSports. We are a dynamic platform where skill meets the blockchain, allowing players to truly own their in-game assets, participate in decentralized governance, and earn real value through competitive play.

This documentation site serves as your comprehensive, official guide to everything FortisArena. Whether you're a new player setting up your first wallet, a seasoned gamer preparing for a major tournament, or a developer exploring our tokenomics, the answers you need are right here.

## What You Will Find Here

| Section | Focus Area |
|:--------|:-----------|
| **Getting Started** | Step-by-step guides on Wallet Setup, account creation, and connecting your digital assets to the game. |
| **The Game & Gameplay** | Detailed information on game mechanics, controls, maps, and advanced strategies for competitive success. |
| **Web3 & Tokenomics** | Deep dives into our native tokens, NFTs (Digital Assets), staking protocols, and the economic structure of the FortisArena ecosystem. |
| **Tournaments & Rules** | Official rulebooks, qualification paths, prize pool structures, and guidelines for fair play in all eSports events. |
| **Security & Support** | Information on account security, anti-cheat measures, and troubleshooting common technical issues. |
