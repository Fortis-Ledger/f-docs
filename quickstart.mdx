## Get Started

Follow these steps to enter the arena and start competing for real rewards.

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

FortisArena uses Web3 technology to secure your identity and assets. You'll need a compatible wallet like **MetaMask**, **Phantom**, or **Trust Wallet**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install a Wallet">
    If you don't have one, install [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/) (for EVM) or [Phantom](https://phantom.app/) (for Solana).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to FortisArena">
    Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button on the top right of the [FortisArena Dashboard](https://fortisarena.io).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Authentication">
    Sign the message in your wallet to verify ownership. This does not cost any gas fees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Join a Tournament

Once authenticated, navigate to the **Active Tournaments** section.

- **Free-to-Play**: Practice your skills without risk.
- **Pay-to-Enter**: Stake tokens to compete for larger prize pools.

### 3. Download the Game Client

For the best experience and anti-cheat protection, download the desktop client.
