# Buying FRT Tokens

FRT is the **access token** for FortisArena—required for tournament entry, organization registration, and unlocking platform features.

<Warning>
  **Not an Investment**
  
  FRT is a **utility token**, not an investment vehicle:
  - ❌ No profit guarantees
  - ❌ No dividend payments
  - ❌ No company ownership
  
  ✅ Access to tournaments  
  ✅ Platform features  
  ✅ Governance (non-financial)
</Warning>

## Buying on PancakeSwap (Step-by-Step)

### What You Need

<Check>
  - [ ] FortisArena account with UID
  - [ ] BNB for gas fees (0.05 BNB enough)
  - [ ] Funds to buy FRT (BNB or BUSD)
</Check>

**Note**: External wallet (MetaMask/Trust) is for temporary use only, then send to Fortis wallet.

### Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get External Wallet (Temporary)">
    Install MetaMask or Trust Wallet just for this purchase. This is only for buying FRT.
    
    <Info>
      This wallet is only for purchase. After buying, send FRT to your Fortis wallet.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get BNB">
    Buy BNB from:
    - Binance (exchange)
    - Any crypto on-ramp
    
    Send to your temporary wallet address.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Visit PancakeSwap">
    Go to [pancakeswap.finance](https://pancakeswap.finance)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Click "Connect Wallet" → Select MetaMask/Trust
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add FRT Token">
    In the swap interface:
    - Pay: BNB
    - Receive: Paste FRT contract address
    
    **FRT Contract:** `0xFf10d933E1Ca7799866B5D2A615e562CAd306c96`
    
    <Warning>
      Always verify contract address! Scammers create fake tokens.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    How much FRT you need:
    - Casual player: 50-100 FRT (~$5-10)
    - Regular competitor: 500+ FRT (~$50+)
    - Organization: 10,000+ FRT (~$1000+)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Swap">
    Click "Swap" → Confirm transaction
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send to Fortis Wallet">
    **IMPORTANT**: Now send FRT to your Fortis wallet:
    
    1. Open Fortis app: Wallet → Receive → Crypto
    2. Copy your deposit address
    3. Send from MetaMask
    4. Arrives in 3-30 minutes
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Direct Deposit to Your UID

### Method: Exchange → Fortis Wallet

If FRT is available on Binance or any exchange:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Deposit Address">
    Fortis app: Wallet → Receive → FRT
    Copy your deposit address (0x...)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Withdraw from Exchange">
    Go to Exchange → Withdraw → FRT
    - Address: Your Fortis deposit address
    - Network: BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)
    - Amount: How much you need
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait">
    3-30 minutes for confirmation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ready">
    FRT appears in your Fortis wallet automatically
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Issues

### "Transaction Failed"

| Problem | Solution |
|:--------|:---------|
| Insufficient BNB | Add 0.05+ BNB for gas |
| Wrong network | Switch to BNB Smart Chain |
| Slippage too low | Increase to 2-3% |

### "Tokens Not Showing"

1. Check BscScan for transaction confirmation
2. Ensure correct network (BSC)
3. Wait 30 minutes max
4. Contact support with tx hash
