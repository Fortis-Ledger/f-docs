# Gameplay Overview

FortisArena is a fast-paced competitive shooter where every match counts. Whether you are a casual player or a pro eSports athlete, the arena offers a mode for you.

## AI-Powered Matchmaking

Our proprietary **Skill-Based Matchmaking (SBMM)** uses AI to analyze your performance in real-time.
- **Fair Testing**: Matches you with opponents of similar reflex speed, accuracy, and win rates.
- **Anti-Smurf**: Rapidly identifies and promotes highly skilled players to higher tiers to prevent griefing.
