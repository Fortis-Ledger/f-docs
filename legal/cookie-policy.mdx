## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide analytical insights.

---

## 2. How We Use Cookies

FortisArena uses cookies for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Cookie Categories

| Type | Purpose | Duration | Can Disable? |
|:-----|:--------|:---------|:-------------|
| **Essential** | Core platform functionality, security, authentication | Session - 1 year | ❌ No |
| **Preferences** | Language, region, display settings | 6 months - 1 year | ✅ Yes |
| **Analytics** | Usage patterns, performance metrics | Up to 2 years | ✅ Yes |
| **Marketing** | Relevant promotions, campaign tracking | Up to 1 year | ✅ Yes |

### 2.2 Specific Uses

**Essential Cookies:**
- Maintain login sessions
- Secure authentication state
- Remember UID (User Identifier) for wallet functions
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Load balance server requests

**Preference Cookies:**
- Language selection
- Theme/display preferences
- Notification settings
- Dashboard customization

**Analytics Cookies:**
- Page visit tracking
- Feature usage statistics
- Error monitoring
- Performance optimization

**Marketing Cookies:**
- Tournament promotion relevance
- Creator spotlight targeting
- Referral tracking
- Campaign effectiveness

## 4. Your Cookie Choices

### 4.1 Managing Preferences

You can control cookies through:

1. **Cookie Banner**: Set preferences on first visit
2. **Settings Page**: Update anytime at Settings → Privacy → Cookies
3. **Browser Settings**: Block cookies at browser level (may affect functionality)

### 4.2 Impact of Disabling

| If You Disable | Effect |
|:---------------|:-------|
| Essential cookies | Platform may not function correctly |
| Preference cookies | Settings reset on each visit |
| Analytics cookies | No impact on functionality |
| Marketing cookies | Less relevant recommendations |
