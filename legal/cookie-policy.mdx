## 1. What Are Cookies?

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on the FortisArena Service. Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to enable websites to function, to improve user experience, and to provide analytical information to the owners of the site.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons, including tracking and analyzing user activity to enhance the Service, secure the platform, and personalize your gaming experience.

| Cookie Type | Description and Purpose | Data Collected | Duration |
|:------------|:----------------------|:---------------|:---------|
| **Strictly Necessary** | Essential for the core operation and security of the Service. They enable features like logging into secure areas, maintaining your wallet connection state, and navigating the site. Cannot be switched off. | Session ID, Server State, Wallet Authentication Tokens (non-private) | Session |
| **Performance and Analytics** | Used to collect information about how you use the Service (e.g., which pages you visit most often, how long you stay, and error messages encountered). This helps us measure and improve performance. | IP address (anonymized), device/browser info, game load times, site metrics | Persistent (Up to 2 Years) |
| **Functionality and Preference** | Used to remember choices you make (such as language, region, username, and custom settings) and to provide more enhanced, personalized features. | Preference settings, region code, previously logged-in username | Persistent (6 Months to 1 Year) |
| **Targeting and Advertising** | Used to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests (both on and off the Service). They also limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns. | User profile data, content consumption, external browsing activity (if consented) | Persistent (Up to 1 Year) |

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies (First-Party), we use third-party cookies from partners such as:

- **Analytics Providers** (e.g., Google Analytics): To analyze traffic and usage patterns.

- **Advertising Networks**: To manage and serve targeted ads.

- **Web3 Infrastructure/Security Partners**: For decentralized service delivery and security checks.

These third parties may have access to your browsing behavior data collected via cookies and are governed by their own respective privacy policies.

## 4. Your Choices and Control

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by:

- **Cookie Consent Banner**: Upon your first visit to the Service, you will be presented with a consent management tool (banner) allowing you to accept all cookies, customize your preferences, or decline non-essential cookies.

- **Browser Settings**: You can modify your browser settings to reject or remove cookies. The procedure for managing cookie settings is different for each browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari). Be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Service, especially those required for wallet connection and secure gaming.

- **Withdrawal of Consent**: You can withdraw your consent for non-essential cookies at any time by accessing the Cookie Preferences link (usually located in the site footer).

## 5. Other Tracking Technologies

We may also use other tracking technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and locally stored objects (LSOs), which are similar to cookies but may store more data. We use these technologies for similar purposes (e.g., to measure the success of marketing campaigns).
